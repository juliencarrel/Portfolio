Game Projects

SOLAR

This toy is a student project, made by my team and I. In it, you can control a sun that can collect other planets in its orbits to grow in size. Your sun's size will decrease after a short time without collecting planets. On this toy, I worked on the art direction of the project, and i created most of the 3D assets and their material. This game has an interesting feature, which is that the player can try to draw whith the trail feedback of the planets in orbit.

7th Continent

In this match 4 game in cooperation or versus, you play as divers who fight to clean up their side of the ocean. You can decide to join your efforts to make the ocean as clean as possible, or to throw your garbage to the other's side. It was made by my team and I on Construct 2 and all the visual assets were made with Photoshop. I especially worked on the artistic direction of the project, including visual assets but also sound and visual feedbacks. I also participated in the Game Design and concept. We wanted to add a touch of burlesque engangement with the topic of ocean pollution to criticize how governments handle this problem.
